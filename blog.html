HISTORY

GULSHAN SANDHU (FOUNDER & CHAIRMAN)

Mr. Sandhu has an interesting history. After getting passed out from Govt. School, he got admission in B.Sc Biotechnology (Dehradun)by chance not by choice. After earning his undergraduate degree in Biotechology, he understood that this was not what he was meant for. So, he decided to pursue his post graduation degree in MBA from I.E.E.T(H.PU). After completing his degree he started his career with banking in IndusInd Bank as a Relationship Officer. After this he joined Seimens as the Head Counselor of North India where he got the exposure and opportunity to work in the field of Marketing developing Automation Labs at very prestigious institutions like IIT’s and NIT’s. After giving 8 years to Seimens, he joined the Education industry considering his own post graduation University- Baddi University of Emerging Sciences Technology as the initial project in 2018. He is heading the Training Placement Department of the university and is continuously nurturing and supporting this organization. He also recognized that it would be very strategic and thoughtful to focus on taking a step further and to promote entrepreneurship, for which he started his own venture of Dairy farm at his hometown. With such sustainability benefits, he is still running that with proper revenue. During the pandemic, a huge section of people lost their jobs and the recent graduates were not even able to opt first job of their career. In such crisis, Mr. Sandhu helped those people by placing more than 1000 people from Hamirpur district in BBN area. From there he recogonised the need to bridge the gap between industry and unemployed people and youth. He found it to be a fabulous first step in everyone’s career to work at larger companies right out of college, as this is how you can learn about industry best practices, seek mentors, and learn from industry experts. To bridge up this gap between the Industry and the potential candidates and to create such opportunities he created a new solution that can potentially be transformative i.e Career Gurus. As they say, there is “no I in team” and the “whole is greater than the sum of its parts”. So, he believes that a leader creates the vision for the future; develops, implements, and continually refines the strategy; and builds, motivates, and inspires an organization to execute that vision.

OBJECTIVE


  • A career vision is a clear mental image of who you want to be and what you want to do in the future.
  • “Your vision is a 'picture' of what you aspire to – and what inspires you – in your work life,”.
  • The main objective of starting this business is to provide sum total counselling to the students from a very initial age, so that the correct alignment can be given to their future career goals. In addition to that, our other objective is to provide ample opportunities for placements by arranging trainings in Aptitude test,LGroup discussions, preparing for Technical and HR interviews through professional trainers even for Diploma and ITI students.
  • Most importantly, to tie up with the industry and bridge the gap between recruiters and candidates, so that the future ready candidates can be provided to them. This will be a sum total collaboration in which guidance and support will also be provided to the entrepreneurs those who want to start their own venture.

CAREER COUNSELLING


  • When students are presented with endless career options to choose from, there comes a time when they have to make a decision but the problem of lack of knowledge and awareness about good career fields stops them from taking any good decision. That’s where career counselling comes to aid.
  • To make career counselling easily accessible to all it is important to know what exactly is it and what are the stages involved. There are broadly three or four stages to a complete career guidance session.
  • Each stage aims at the specific outcomes to be arrived at which could be the input to the next phase of evaluation. This would allow each part of the client to be focused upon and to be improved to facilitate the taking up of an active career option.
